About Our Entrepreneurship Class

The **Earn While You Learn (EWYL)** initiative at **Maharaja's College, Ernakulam** is transforming education for economically disadvantaged students. Launched by the Higher Education Department of Kerala, our programme enables students to gain practical skills while earning an income.

Students manage every aspect of our eco-friendly ventures—from production to marketing—developing entrepreneurial skills that complement their academic studies. Each purchase directly supports student education and sustainable practices.

This market is the culmination of our students' term project in practical entrepreneurship. Each student researched, designed, prototyped, and produced their unique product, focusing on sustainable practices and high-quality craftsmanship.

By purchasing an item, you are directly funding the students' future educational activities and supporting the growth of young local makers. Thank absolutely you for making a difference!
